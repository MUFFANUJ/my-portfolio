About Me

Frontend Developer

Hey there! I'm Anuj, a passionate B.Tech student in Computer Science and AI with a keen eye for crafting beautiful and functional user interfaces. While I'm knee-deep in algorithms and neural networks by day, my evenings are dedicated to bringing websites and apps to life with code and creativity.

My journey with frontend development began with a simple spark - witnessing the magic of transforming lines of code into sleek, user-friendly interfaces. Since then, I've been hooked! I love the challenge of translating complex designs into pixel-perfect reality, ensuring every interaction feels smooth and intuitive.

My tech bag includes my trusty arsenal of frontend tools like JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. But beyond the frameworks, I believe the true power lies in understanding users and their needs. That's why I approach every project with empathy, striving to create interfaces that are not just visually appealing, but also user-centered and impactful.
